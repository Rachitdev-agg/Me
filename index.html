Writing

I have always been really into reading. When I was a kid and couldn't read, I would make my parents reads story books to me. After some time I learnt how to read myself and discovered a whole world of fiction and fantasy. Then as I was reading harry potter for the 10th time, I thought that I should write as well. And after many failed attempts at writing, I have finnaly found my style. Its aesthetic writing. I dont even know if thats a real type of writing but its just what I call it. And now I sometimes write when I am too full of feelings. Writing has become a way for me to release my feelings and exposing them to people without really exposing them to people.
